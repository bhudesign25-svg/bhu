Assisted Living
Project @ Chennai

Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Dedicated to providing a dignified and comfortable living environment for senior citizens, this project in Chennai integrates accessibility with aesthetic warmth. The design focuses on communal well-being while ensuring individual privacy and ease of movement. Light-filled spaces, safety-conscious features, and serene landscape pockets create a nurturing habitat that supports both physical and emotional health. It is a space designed not just for living, but for thriving.
