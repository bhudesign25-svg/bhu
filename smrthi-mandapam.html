Smrthi Mandapam

Kayamkulam, Kerala

A monolithic expression of memory and time, the Smrthi Mandapam in Kayamkulam is dedicated to Adv. CR Jayaprakash, Ex. KPCC General Secretary. The architectural narrative unfolds through a play of light and shadow, creating a dialogue between the built form and the surrounding landscape. Envisaged as a pavilion of peace, the structure employs a palette of natural stone and timber to create a timeless aesthetic. The spatial organization guides the visitor through a journey of homage, anchoring the site as a landmark of local history and architectural clarity.
